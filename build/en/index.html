| Authentication

Create an account or log in. Registration is quick and easy.

| Add a collaborator

Easily invite team members to Pro Gestion Soft to collaborate and share responsibilities. You can assign roles to each collaborator, granting access to the sections they need to contribute effectively.

| Delete your account

If you decide to stop using Pro Gestion Soft, you can permanently delete your account. This action is irreversible and will remove all your data from our system. Make sure to back up important information before proceeding.

| Verify your account

To ensure the security of your account, we will send a verification email after registration. Click on the link in the email to verify your address. This step is essential to activate your account and ensure secure access.

| Add an email

To enhance security and communication, you can add an additional email address to your account. This feature allows you to receive important notifications and improve account recovery if needed.

| Manage applications

Simplify your recruitment process by tracking and managing applications directly on Pro Gestion Soft. Post job openings, evaluate candidates, and follow every step of the recruitment process. Access CVs and evaluation notes to make informed decisions.
